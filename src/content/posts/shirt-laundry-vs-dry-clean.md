Most **cotton and cotton-blend** dress shirts are happiest with **laundry and press**: water-based cleaning removes perspiration salts and skin oils, then a press restores a crisp collar and cuff.

## Good candidates for shirt laundry

- Standard broadcloth and pinpoint oxfords  
- Shirts you wear weekly with no “dry clean only” label  
- Pieces where you want a **starch** level from light to heavy  

## When dry cleaning is safer

- Shirts with **satin panels**, unusual blends, or fragile dyes  
- Extreme stains that need solvent-specific spotting  
- Designer pieces with strict care labels  

We will match the process to the label and the fabric so collars stay smooth without shine marks.
